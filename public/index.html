Football Players API

Introduction

Welcome! This is a simple API of football that can be used for fetching data in football related projects.So, the thing is I searched up in internet for different players datasets and I found a perfect dataset for this api. Then I added that dataset here and I took that data, compiled it into objects to make different routes and again turned it into json for sending the data. Different routes and their features are listed below.

Routes & Features

  1. /api/players - Fetches all 17954 players.
  2. /api/players/:name - Fetches any player by specific name.
  3. /api/top/:n - Get top n players by rating.
  4. /api/country/:country - Get players by country
  5. /api/position/:position - Get players by specific position
  6. /api/rating/:rating - Get players by specific rating
  7. /api/random - Get a random player
  8. /api/nationality-list - Get list of nationalities
  9. /api/top-dribblers - Get top 10 dribblers
  10. /api/top-pacers - Get top 10 pacers
  11. /api/top-passers - Get top 10 passers
  12. /api/top-finishers - Get top 10 finishers
  13. /api/best-value - Get best valued players
  14. /api/top10 - Get top 10 players
